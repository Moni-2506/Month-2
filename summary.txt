This project analyzes Netflix content to identify trends in movies and TV shows.

First, I cleaned the dataset by handling missing values and converting date columns into datetime format.
Then, I performed feature engineering by creating variables like content_type, release_decade, and extracting year-wise data.

In the visualization phase:
- I used a pie chart to compare Movies vs TV Shows.
- A line chart shows rapid growth of content after 2015.
- Bar charts highlight top genres like Drama and Comedy.
- A heatmap shows which countries produce the most content.

From the analysis, I found that Netflix is heavily movie-focused but is increasingly investing in TV shows.
Also, content production is concentrated in a few countries like the US and India.

Finally, I built a simple prediction model which suggests content will continue growing in the next 2 years.
